## Recommended Presentation Structure

For a typical AGIC report or pitch:

1. **Cover** — slide 29 or 3 (branded, looks polished)
2. **Agenda** — slide 5 or 6 (simple, text-only)
3. **Section divider** — slide 10 or 12 (AGIC-branded section header)
4. **Content slides** — slides 15–19 (varied: image+text, multi-column, icon grid)
5. **Data / diagrams** — slide 37
6. **Team / photos** — slides 19, 24, or 35
7. **Closing** — slide 38 or 39
